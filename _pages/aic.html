Inline formula: $c_{\mathcal{A}}(i) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{j=1}^{N} S(i,j)$ Display formula: $$ S(i,j) = - \log_2 \sum_{\{p(i,j)\}} P[p(i,j)], \quad P[p(i,j)] = \frac{1}{d_i} \prod_{l \neq i \neq j \in p(i,j)} \frac{1}{d_l - 1}. $$
